We Eat, We Share, We Care

The WeEat Mission Statement

We started “We Eat” to help our sisters and brothers in need. We have to be reminded to give thanks for the blessings that we receive each and everyday. Far too many of our friends don’t have food, clothes and shelter. While we as a country have accumulated the largest amount of wealth in the history of our species, many have been left behind. Our goal is simple; we are here to provide assistance to those in need, regardless of their personal stories. We are Heart Centered and our assistance comes from a loving energy which permeates the entire human condition and indeed the Cosmos. Our motto, “We Eat, We Share, We Care” , is our mission statement. We intend to grow and integrate into neighborhoods throughout our great country. The foundation of our work will be the kindness and compassion of like minded souls who have an abiding love for all of humanity. We reach out to all of our friends from a place of humility and love. Please help us to help those in need. Open your hearts and wallets to make someone’s life a little better.

Our Work

School Breakfast Program

Millions of kids in America face the school day on an empty stomach because there isn't enough food at home, but a healthy school breakfast program can help.

Afternoon Meal Program

For some kids, lunch at school may be the last meal of the day; an WeEat afterschool meals program is their best hope. Learn how you can help.

Summer Meal Program

For kids who rely on school meals, summer is a tough time. A free summer meals program for kids at a school, church or community center is the answer.

Family Nutrition Education Programs

Through our family nutrition education programs, we're helping parents and caregivers learn how to shop and cook healthy, affordable meals.

Give Today

Every day in New Jersey, 1 in 4 preschool-aged children live unsure of their next meal. Your generour donation helps us deliver not just a healthy meal, but hope.
